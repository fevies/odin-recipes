STRAWBERRY BISCUITS

these strawberry biscuits have been in the family for over 40 years.It is the best biscuit to have under your cream dessert

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 white sugar
  • 3 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cup milk

Directions

step 1

preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.Grease a 12 cup muffin tin.

step 2

mix flour ,sugar ,baking powder and salt together in a bowl ,cut in shortening and mix to combine

step 3

Beat eggs in a separate bowl and mix in the milk to combine.Add to the dry ingredients and mix until moistened.pour batter evenly into each of the prepared muffin cups and transfer to the oven as quick as possible

step 4

Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown,about 20 minutes
